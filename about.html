Perspective

Edge Studio is a multimedia concept art and visual development house focused on world-building and IP development.

Led by some of the industry’s fiercest, most impassioned creatives, we create designs that are thoughtful, engaging and beautiful. We breathe life into irresistible new worlds.

BIG, POWERFULLY ORIGINAL IDEAS We raise new worlds from the void, set them on their axis, and spin them with histories, cultures, ecosystems and technologies. Through thoughtful design and close collaboration, our artwork pulls people in and compels them to care—even obsess.
BETTER TEAMS, BETTER WORK We build specialized strike teams for each project. Drawn from our global roster of five-star talent, our teams are hyper-focused on efficiency, consistency and quality to hit the ground running every time. We were one of the first in our industry to pioneer this approach when we started in 2008. And to this day, we’re still the best at it.
